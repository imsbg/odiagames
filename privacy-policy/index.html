Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 03, 2025

Your privacy is extremely important to us at Odia Games ('we', 'us', or 'our'). This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by odiagames.site and how we use it. This policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in Odia Games.

1. Information We Absolutely Do Not Collect

We have designed our website to be as privacy-friendly as possible. We want you to play with peace of mind. Therefore, we DO NOT collect, store, or ask for any of the following personal identification information:

  • Your Email Address
  • Your Password
  • Your IP Address
  • Your Physical Address or Location Data

2. Information We Do Collect

To provide a functional and competitive gaming experience, we collect a very limited amount of non-personal data:

  • Multiplayer Game Data: For multiplayer games (like Tic Tac Toe, Bingo, etc.), we store your game-related data, such as your chosen username (which is not linked to any personal info), game scores, and match history. This is essential for features like leaderboards and to connect you with other players.

3. Single-Player vs. Multiplayer Games

There is a clear distinction in how we handle data between game types:

  • Single-Player Games: All data, progress, and scores for single-player games are stored locally on your device (in your browser). This information is never sent to our servers, shared, or stored by us.
  • Multiplayer Games: As stated above, only gameplay and score data are stored on our servers to enable the multiplayer functionality.

4. Third-Party Advertising

We use third-party advertising to keep our website free. These ads are only displayed on our home page and never during gameplay.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks may use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see. Note that Odia Games has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited game data we store from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the 'Last Updated' date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at message.sbg@gmail.com.
