About Us

Welcome to Odia Games! We are a passionate team based in Dhenkanal, Odisha, dedicated to reviving the cherished games of our childhood. This is a unique game website where you can find and play our traditional games in both Odia and English.

We are proud to be the first website in Odisha where you can enjoy these nostalgic games in our own mother tongue, connecting our generation and the next with our rich cultural roots.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve, promote, and digitally archive the iconic games of Odisha. In a world of fast-paced digital entertainment, we aim to provide a space where the simplicity and joy of our traditional games can be experienced by anyone, anywhere. We believe that games are a powerful way to keep our culture and language alive.

Meet the Founder

Odia Games was founded by Sandeep Biswal G, a tech enthusiast with a deep love for his culture. His vision is to create a high-quality platform that not only entertains but also serves as a bridge to our heritage. You can follow his journey on Instagram.

What's Next?

The journey has just begun! In the future, we will be adding more iconic Odia games that we all grew up playing on boards and in the fields. We are constantly working to improve the experience and expand our collection of games.
